Problem to solve

Problem — is a huge waste of time and money. Software development companies and teams waste their time and money flying blind if they don't instrument and use Product Intelligence for their products. And this includes not only Product Marketing analytics (like Google Analytics) but also Product analytics.

Practice shows [1][2][3] that in average only about 15% of features is being used by 80% of highly paying customers. And the problem is that companies and teams without product usage analytics don't know which 15% of their product features are being used by those paying customers — and they have to spend their time, money and energy on supporting those 85% of features that no one or only free users use.

This 19-th century quote is still true if we don't instrument and use analytics for our products Marketing:

"I am convinced that about one-half the money I spend for advertising is wasted, but I have never been able to figure out which half" [i]

And we have the same problem with uninstrumented Product and Features development. This is a huge waste on product design, development, testing, operations and management — which leads to degradation of customers UX, higher customers churn, lower upsales, etc. — it leads to even more waste.

Practice shows [1] that about 80% of teams don't make (and if make then don't use) tooling for Product Analytics.
