Case studies

Google Analytics app

Google Analytics app helps to understand if there any visitors on website, where do they come from (both geographycally and from any referring web resource), how much time do they spend on web site, do they visit links in the menu, do they "convert" over "App Store" and "Google Play" links and how much.

This information helps development teams to optimize this web site for the puproses it was created:

  • convert visitors into customers;
  • connect customers with support;
  • provide visitors with open job positions;
  • provide visitors of any kind with more information about the company.

Do They Use app

Do They Use app helps to understand if users use all the elements on each screen of the app, how much do they use and who of them namely. It helps to understand which options are selected the most, which controls are most popular and which not, etc.

This information helps development teams to get rid of elements and options that never being used and to focus optimization of UX for the different kinds of users and users groups to grow the business:

  • already buing customers;
  • free users who eventually convert to buying customers;
  • free users who never convert to buying customers, but who ambasador and advocate for the app and help to bring more new buying customers.

Development teams get the insights on what could be improved in the app based on real usage data that helps to grow the business as well. They don't just optimize. They optimize for the categories of customers they see really use the app.
